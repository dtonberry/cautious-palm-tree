Overview

The land of Aetheria is full of magic and life.

Explore caves, dungeons and lively towns to fulfil your goal.



Controls

With both touch and key controls, you choose how you experience your adventure in Aetheria.

Key controls with ultilise WASD/Arrow keys

Touch controls will use an onscreen d-pad and action buttons



Gameplay

During his adventure, Mathew will have to fight enemies and solve puzzles

There will be many different enemies and puzzles to fight and solve.



Art Design

Tales of Adventure uses 2D sprites and spritesheets for animation.



Sound Design

Tales of Adventure uses a chiptune soundtrack



Game Engine

Tales of Adventure is being developed using the Phaser 3 game framework, a JavaScript based Game Engine.
